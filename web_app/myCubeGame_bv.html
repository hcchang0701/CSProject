Welcome to my first web game!
Before you start, please read the following instructions first.

  1. Click one of the three buttons below that indicates size of the board to start a game, you will see pairs of colored blocks with a black frame embedded in, here we call them "odd" blocks. For those which are not odd blocks, are labelled as "even".
  2. Your goal is to match all the pairs through white blocks without any intersections.
  3. Once you click on an odd block, you are able and only be able to color the even blocks with its color by clicking them as well. If you click on an odd block with the same color, you will be incapable of coloring other blocks.
  4. As you connected a pair, the shortest path between the pair would be highlighted by white border.
  5. If you successfully completed the level or just found it too hard for you, you could take another challenge by clicking the size buttons below. Gameplay record will only be collected upon finishing.
  6. The more you play, the more grateful I am!
    Enjoy and have fun!
